    Diaries of Chinese Streets

    "The idea for this came to me when I was on a train home as a sentence I heard from a stranger that day kept repeating inside my head. It was something a mother said to her son, and for some very weird reason, it stuck with me as I moved on with my life and they moved on with theirs, with the slightest possibility of having our paths crossing again.

    This happened more than once, with different words flying around from strangers whose lives cross mine only for very few seconds, and the idea to write their -rather mundane, and can I dare say 'insignificant?' words nagged inside my head.

    And then it grew, into moments I collected that put a little color to my ordinary days."
