    About Me

    Hello! I'm Aaliyah, a 27-year-old Egyptian living in China. My native language is Arabic, and I'm fluent in English and Chinese. My journey with Chinese began in 2014 when I first heard the language. Unlike many, I instantly fell in love with how it sounded. That fascination stayed with me through my final school years until, in 2016, I decided to pursue a bachelor's degree in Chinese Language and Literature. As a writer, I was often frustrated by my inability to fully express my thoughts in English. I’ve always felt that English, as a language, sometimes fails to capture the depth of emotion and thought I want to convey. So, I searched for a language with more layers, and what better choice than what’s often considered the mother of Asian languages?
